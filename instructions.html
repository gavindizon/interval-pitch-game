Instructions:

  1. To move, you may use the traditional W, A, S, & D keys.
  2. The main objective of this game is to identify pitch intervals by listening to a sequence of notes. Hitting the correct notes would help you unlock hidden areas to further progress through the level.
  3. The notes and their respective keys are as follows:
    1. Do - U
    2. Re - I
    3. Mi - O
    4. Fa - P
    5. Sol - J
    6. La - K
    7. Ti - L
    8. Do (Octave) - U+Shift
    9. Re (Octave) - I+Shift
    10. Mi (Octave) - O+Shift
    11. Fa (Octave) - P+Shift
    12. Sol (Octave) - J+Shift
    13. La (Octave) - K+Shift
    14. Ti (Octave) - L+Shift
